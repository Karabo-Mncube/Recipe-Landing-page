Easy Recipe for a perfect pasta dish!

Create a delicious pasta dish with these easy steps and share it with your close friends and family. This Creamy Mushroom Pasta speaks for itself. A slippery tangle of pasta bathed in a creamy parmesan sauce loaded with buttery garlic mushrooms

Ingredients

minced garlic
diced onion
sliced mushrooms
3 tablespoons of olive oil
1/4 teaspoon of salt
a pack of spaghetti
1 cup grated parmesan cheese
3/4 Cup of heavy cream
dried parsley

Instructions

  1. Boil your pasta in a separate pot with boiling water
  2. heat some olive oil in a pan and fry your diced onion,sliced mushroom and minced garlic
  3. add your cup of heavy cream and parmesan cheese and season it with salt
  4. once your pasta is soft and ready, discard the water and add it in your sauce mixture
  5. serve and garnish with parsley

On the side

Serve this creamy pasta with a side of garlic bread or cheesy garlic bread. If you're going for something light, you can serve this dish with a side of a fresh, crisp salad

ENJOY!!!
